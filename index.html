Bio

  • I am currently a 5th Ph.D. candidate in the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) under the supervision of Prof. Hui Xiong. I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from USTC in 2017. I have also spent time at Talent Intelligent Center ,Baidu Inc. as a Research Intern from 2017 to 2022 under the supervision of Dr. Hengshu Zhu.
  • My general research interest includes data mining and machine learning, focusing on generative models, variation inference, graph neural networks, and their data intensive applications.
